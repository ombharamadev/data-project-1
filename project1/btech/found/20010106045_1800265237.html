Himachal Pradesh Technical University (HIMTU)

Posted on April 10, 2024
By Saurabh Agarwal
Update- Himachal Pradesh Technical University is a Government University in Hamirpur, Himachal Pradesh, India, established in 2011. Updates of Himachal Pradesh Technical University Results and Himtu results name wise available exclusively at Indiaresult. Check Himachal Pradesh Technical University Exam Results at himtu.ac.in. Prof. Prem Kumar Dhumal Hon'ble Chief Minister of Himachal Pradesh has announced the establishment of Himachal Pradesh Technical University with the objective to create excellent competent environment to impart the technical education across the State. Get your HPTU Btech Results 2021 with all details. HPTU affiliates many colleges in the state of Himachal Pradesh. The Himachal Pradesh Technical University is established with the objectives for value creation and welfare in society through technical education and training, research, innovation, public and private sector consultancy, entrepreneurship, continuing education programmes, autonomous Colleges/Institution and affiliating constituent and private Colleges/Institutions.Himachal Pradesh Technical University results are available on Indiaresults.com as well.
